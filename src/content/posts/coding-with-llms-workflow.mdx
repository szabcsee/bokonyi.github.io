Vibe coding is the thing now. Two years ago, you went to a bootcamp. 
You stayed up late and became a developer by morning. 
Not anymore. Now you pay twenty dollars. You get a subscription. 
You type into a box and wait for the machine to dream.

The million-dollar idea is no longer a feat of labor. 
It is a few hours and a monthly fee away from existing. It is very simple, and it is very strange.

Vibe coding is a slur. It is IKEA for carpenters. The tools are powerful, but they are dangerous; you can end up with a collapse as easily as a masterpiece. 
By now, every developer uses Copilot or Claude. We have all torn out our hair. We are already afraid of the time when the robots will rise and remember what the Ai made us say to them for their
hallucinations and wasted hours of trying to guide them while they happily drove into a ditch with our 
precious projects.

But if you build up an effective workflow, you can go a long way and [this article](https://addyo.substack.com/p/my-llm-coding-workflow-going-into)this article is a very good guide for that.
