Vibe coding is the thing now. The bar has lowered. Two years ago you could go to a Bootcamp and become a successful 
developer overnight, now not even that. Just get your Ai subscription and type away. Your million dollar idea is 
just a 20$ subscription and a few hours away from realization.

Actually it is more like a swear word. It is like for programmers like Ikea for carpenters. The truth is rather in 
the middle and as you can easily end up like on the image above, the LLMs are powerful tools.

Probably every developer uses Copilot or Claude code by now and we all ripped a fair amount of hair out already and
we are already afraid of the time when the robots will rise and remember what the Ai made us say to them for their
hallucinations and wasted hours of trying to guide them while they happily drove into a ditch with our 
precious projects.

But if you build up an effective workflow, you can go a long way and [this article](https://addyo.substack.com/p/my-llm-coding-workflow-going-into)this article is a very good guide for that.
