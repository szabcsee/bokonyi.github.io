I went on daily.dev today that was my go to site for quite sometime, but recently
it became such a clickbait full off affiliates and AI trash, that I gave up my OCD fueled 
streaks and rarely visit it. But time to time a good blogpost catches my attention and today was one of 
[Himanshu Singour's](https://medium.com/@himanshusingour7/i-gave-2-hours-daily-to-dsa-system-design-best-decision-ever-af6c0ae6a938) about how he invested daily 2 hours in System Design for a 
month and it was the best decision. It really hit home for me because it is just such a simple yet powerful thing to do.
There is really nothing more powerful than habits and there is nothing that can change your life more than changing your habits.
